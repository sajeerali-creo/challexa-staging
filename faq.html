1. What is Challexa?
+
Challexa is a competitive racket sports platform where players join tournaments, compete in ladder challenges, track rankings, and build their player profile. It is designed to make competitive play structured, fair, and exciting.
2. What sports does Challexa support?
+
Challexa currently focuses on:
  • • Padel
  • • Tennis
  • • Pickleball
  • • (Future expansion: other racket sports)
3. How do I join a Challexa tournament?
+
  • 1. Register through the official Challexa registration link.
  • 2. Complete payment (if applicable).
  • 3. Receive confirmation and tournament details.
  • 4. Check-in on event day (QR or desk check-in).
4. What tournament formats does Challexa use?
+
Depending on the event:
  • • Group Stage + Knockout
  • • Round Robin
  • • King of the Court (social format)
  • • One-off competitive events
Each tournament page clearly states the format before registration.
5. How does the Group Stage + Knockout format work?
+
  • • Teams are divided into groups.
  • • Each team plays round-robin matches within their group.
  • • Top teams qualify for Knockout rounds.
  • • Winners advance until a Champion is crowned.
    • Tie-breakers may include:
  • • Match wins
  • • Set difference
  • • Head-to-head result
  • • Point difference
6. What is the Challexa Challenge Ladder?
+

The Challenge Ladder allows players or teams to:

  • • Challenge opponents above them
  • • Move up the rankings
  • • Earn tier progression
  • • Compete continuously throughout the season

It creates ongoing competition beyond one-off tournaments.
