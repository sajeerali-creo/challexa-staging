Effective Date: [Insert Date]

1. Introduction

Challexa respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data.

This Privacy Policy explains:

  • What data we collect
  • How we use it
  • How we protect it

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Profile photo

2.2 Competition Data

  • Match results
  • Rankings
  • Badges
  • Tournament participation

2.3 Technical Data

  • Device information
  • IP address
  • Usage activity

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide platform services
  • Manage tournaments and ladders
  • Display rankings and profiles
  • Communicate updates and notifications
  • Improve platform performance

4. Data Sharing

We may share data with:

  • Clubs and organizers (competition-related only)
  • Service providers (hosting, analytics)

We do not sell personal data.

5. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained while your account is active
  • Competition history may remain for ranking integrity
  • Users may request deletion where legally permitted

6. Data Security

We implement:

  • Secure servers
  • Access controls
  • Encryption where applicable

However, no system is 100% secure.

7. User Rights

Depending on jurisdiction, you may:

  • Access your data
  • Request corrections
  • Request deletion
  • Withdraw consent

8. Cookies

Challexa uses cookies to:

  • Maintain sessions
  • Improve user experience

You may disable cookies via browser settings.

9. International Use

Data may be processed in different countries depending on hosting and services used. Appropriate safeguards are applied.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated to users.
